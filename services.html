General Questions
Isn’t it expensive?

Compared to the $150 that you could pay for a logo from a non-professional freelance designer on Fiverr, yes it is.

It’s important to stand out as a brand to be able to position yourself as a premium product or a service. Getting a strong brand identity gives you the opportunity to be taken seriously by large organizations, builds trust, and conveys stability. It shows commitment and scale that is directly reflected by the user.

No matter how great of a product you have, the brand identity can be the component that will either push away the client or sell your product. With our experience as creatives, we take all these things in mind, research your professional field, and deliver a high-quality, premium brand identity that will be a perfect starting point for your company.

What if I don't like any of the proposed design directions?

If such a rare case happens there are two options:

A. Continue work with us within a new, extended timeframe and additional budget of €600, which will guarantee you at least two more logo directions.

B. Cancel the project and not pay the second instalment - the remaining 50% of the payment. This way you won’t be able to use any of the design assets we have already created.

How to download an item?

Once you've purchased your items, we recommend that you download them immediately as items may be removed from Envato Market from time to time.

To download your item:

  1. Hover the mouse over your username at the top of the screen and click 'Downloads’ from the drop down menu.
  2. Click the 'Download' button next to the item.
  3. Click ‘Main File(s)’ which contains all source files.
  4. Click ‘Licence Certificate and Purchase Code’ for licence information only.
If you haven’t already confirmed your email address, please check your inbox, or resend the email via the link in your download page.
Process
What do I need to prepare in order to start the 5-day design sprint with you?

First, we ask you to fill out our smart questionnaire form. The main thing we get from your answers is what is your own perception of your future brand. This will give us a direction of thinking and research.

The form has some additional questions that are not directly related to the visual identity but their answers will give us the whole picture about the plans for your brand—what problem do you solve and who is your customer (target audience).

Second, we will need a list of identities you like and ones you don't like—and why. A list of your direct competitors will make it easier for both you and us to get on the same page faster. If you have a pitch deck already created, don't hesitate to share it with us.

I need a few changes after the visual identity is completed

The 5-days design sprints are designed in a super optimal way to make it possible to deliver high-quality visual identity. A huge part of that process is the collaboration with you - the stakeholder. This means that during the 5-day design sprint you must be available to review our proposals, discuss it with us and your team, and give us feedback within the stated timeframes. We will make this process as easy as possible for you.

What is the payment process?

The payment is split into two parts - 50% due to the beginning of a project, 50% upon delivery, no exceptions. Yes, it's a streamlined process too.

Before we set the kick-off date, we have a contract created and signed, and you should have the first payment of €1000 made.

Then, on the delivery Day 5 (usually on Friday), the second payment is being made right before we send you the entire package with ready-to-use and source files.

Services & Partnerships
I need a few changes after the visual identity is completed

Once we deliver all of the materials defined by the Predesign™ Package, we can make additional changes on the identity and its materials only as a separate contract, based on a custom quote, defined by your request and needs.

A piece of advice, before going for any change, collect data - interview people and get feedback, analyze the industry that you are in, etc. This will make the outcome of the next design iteration more efficient.
